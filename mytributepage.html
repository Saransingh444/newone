MAHENDRA SINGH DHONI

The man behind every success(THALA)

Here Is A Time Line Of M S Dhoni's Life:

  • DEC 2004 - Dhoni makes his international debut against Bangladesh in an ODI at Chittagong.
  • OCT 2005 - Promoted up the order to accelerate scoring, Dhoni smashes an unbeaten 183 off 145 balls, the then record of the highest individual score in ODI cricket in the second innings..
  • DEC 2005 - Dhoni makes his Test debut against Sri Lanka in Chennai..
  • SEP 2007 - Dhoni takes over the reins of ODI captaincy from Rahul Dravid..
  • AUG 2008 - Dhoni leads India to its first-ever bilateral ODI series victory in Sri Lanka..
  • NOV 2008 - Dhoni makes his debut as full-time Test captain of India during the fourth and final Test against Australia at Nagpur.
  • DEC 2008 - Dhoni wins the ICC ODI Player of the Year honour..
  • MAR 2009 - Dhoni leads India to its first bilateral ODI series victory in New Zealand..
  • APR 2009 - Dhoni honoured with the Padma Shri, India's fourth highest civilian honour..
  • DEC 2009 - Under Dhoni's captaincy, India tops the ICC Test cricket rankings for the first time..
  • DEC 2009 - Dhoni retains his ICC ODI Player of the year award, becomes the first player to win it twice..
  • MAY 2010 - Dhoni leads Chennai Super Kings to their maiden Indian Premier League title..
  • APR 2018 - May 2018: Under Dhoni's captaincy, Chennai Super Kings win their third IPL title by defeating Sunrisers Hyderabad by eight wickets..
